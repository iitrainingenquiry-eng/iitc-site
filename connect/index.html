Company Overview

II Training & Consultancy (IITC) is a corporate learning and workforce capability development firm dedicated to helping organisations build high-performing, future-ready teams. As a Registered Training Provider with HRD Corp Malaysia, IITC delivers structured and impact-driven learning programs designed to strengthen leadership capability, enhance workforce productivity, and support sustainable organisational growth.

Our approach goes beyond training delivery, aligning initiatives with business strategy for measurable operational, environmental, and social impact.
